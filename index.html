Saving Time & Money through Boring-Task-Automation
2016-12-12

When I started at my current employer I went through a new onboarding process of sitting down with different departments for the first week including sales, and support. For the first time in my career I was able to see how archaic some processes were on the other side.
Every Monday, my co-worker would sit down and break apart large, several-hundred and sometimes thousand line excel documents into smaller individual reports to email off to people. This would take him 4-6 hours to do, for just one.
At the time I was dabbling with Python, and I figured I could make this task much easier for him. It was a pretty simple workflow: Get CSV, Split master CSV into multiple CSVs based off matching values in a single column and save. Easy.
So I went to work with the little Python I knew and within 2 days I had a little CLI program that he can run every Monday and now his very long, expensive reporting process now takes him no more than 5 minutes to do.
He has since used this script to break up other reports saving him, and the company, hundreds of hours and thousands of dollars in labour each year.
It is instances like this that really bring me joy in software development. I love being able to build tools to help other people do their job better, and faster.
